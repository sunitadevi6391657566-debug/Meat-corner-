# **App Name**: Meat Corner - Trace & Fresh

## Core Features:

- User Authentication & Profile Management: Enables customers and sellers to securely sign up, log in via OTP/Google, and manage their basic profiles.
- Location-Based Live Seller Feed: Detects user's location to display live video streams from nearby sellers in a short-video format, including distance, price, and other key details.
- Seller Live Broadcasting & Product Setup: Allows sellers to initiate live video streams of their meat/animals, and input real-time details like type, weight, price, quantity, and cutting time.
- Customer Real-time Booking System: Facilitates customers in viewing live streams, selecting desired quantity, placing orders with advance or COD payment options, and choosing between pickup or delivery.
- AI-Powered Content Safety Moderation Tool: An AI tool to automatically blur sensitive body parts, issue '18+ Sensitive Content' warnings, detect illegal content, and support user reporting.
- Seller Registration & Verification: A streamlined process for sellers to register their shop details, upload business licenses, and await admin review and approval.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep, rich red (#7D0000) that evokes quality and freshness while establishing the requested 'Dark Red Theme'. This will be used for prominent elements and interactive components.
- Background color: A very dark, desaturated reddish-grey (#211C1C) to serve as a subtle yet distinctly themed backdrop for the app, aligning with the dark scheme.
- Accent color: A vibrant, light magenta-rose (#FBB8EC) chosen to stand out prominently against the dark primary and background colors. Ideal for 'Live badges', animated buttons, and other calls to action to create a sense of urgency and activity.
- Headline and body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for clean, modern readability that complements the smooth and fast user experience.
- User interfaces will utilize 'Rounded cards' for content display and information encapsulation, offering a soft, modern aesthetic.
- The home screen will feature a 'Short-video feed style' layout, optimized for vertical scrolling and mirroring the familiar experience of platforms like Instagram Reels.
- Smooth, subtle animations for screen transitions, element interactions like 'Animated buttons', and content loading, enhancing the overall user experience and contributing to a 'Smooth' and 'Fast' feel.
- Crisp, modern iconography will be used throughout the app, with specific emphasis on prominent 'Live badges' to indicate active streams and draw user attention.
